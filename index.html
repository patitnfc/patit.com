SafeTag is an NFC-enabled keychain designed to ensure your child’s safety. It securely stores critical details like emergency contacts and medical information. In case your child is lost, a simple tap of SafeTag on an NFC-enabled smartphone provides trusted individuals with the information needed to reunite them with you quickly.

TagBuddy is an NFC-enabled locket that attaches to your pet’s collar, storing vital information like your contact details and the pet’s medical records. If your pet goes missing, anyone who finds them can simply tap their smartphone against the locket to access the information and help reunite you quickly. Durable, waterproof, and easy to update, TagBuddy offers a high-tech, reliable way to keep your pet safe.

SocialTap is an NFC-enabled card designed to make sharing your social media presence seamless and instant. With just one tap, this sleek, compact card can open any of your social media profiles directly on a smartphone. Simply tap the card against an NFC-enabled device, and it will instantly redirect to your chosen social media account, eliminating the need to type or scan QR codes.

TapTable is an NFC-enabled table scanner designed to enhance the dining and service experience for restaurants and businesses. With a simple tap of a smartphone, customers can instantly access menus, place orders, make payments, or provide feedback without the need for physical menus or waiting for staff assistance.

ReviewTap is an NFC-enabled solution designed to simplify customer feedback for restaurants, cafes, and businesses. Available as table scanners, cards, or keychains, ReviewTap allows customers to leave reviews effortlessly. With a quick tap of their NFC-enabled smartphone, customers are directed to a feedback form or review platform, eliminating the need for manual searches or links.
