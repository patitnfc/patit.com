SafeTag: Your Child’s Safety Keychain

SafeTag is an NFC-enabled keychain designed to ensure your child’s safety. It securely stores critical details like emergency contacts and medical information. In case your child is lost, a simple tap of SafeTag on an NFC-enabled smartphone provides trusted individuals with the information needed to reunite them with you quickly.
